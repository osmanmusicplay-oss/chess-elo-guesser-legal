By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Who we are

"Chess Elo Guesser is operated by an independent publisher based in Turkey. For privacy requests, contact us at kuponai.app@gmail.com."

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of information:

  • Leaderboard and nickname data. If you choose to register a public nickname, we store that nickname together with a pseudonymous device identifier we assign on your device, game mode, scores, and related session metadata. This is used to display rankings and to enforce fair-use rules (for example, rate limiting). We do not require a password or email for basic play with a nickname; your nickname is not verified as uniquely “yours” beyond our technical rules.
  • Locally stored identifiers and preferences. The App may save a random player identifier, nickname, accessibility or audio preferences (such as music mute and volume), and similar settings on your device using local storage or equivalent APIs.
  • Gameplay and technical data. When you interact with our backend (for example, Supabase or similar services), standard technical data may be processed automatically, such as timestamps, IP addresses, device/browser characteristics as observed by the service, and request metadata needed for security, abuse prevention, and reliability.
  • Third-party content. The App may load chess games or related metadata from datasets or services you configure. It may also load fonts, libraries, or media from third-party hosts (for example, standard sound assets as referenced in the App configuration). Those providers may collect data under their own policies and HTTP logs.

We do not intentionally collect precise real-time location. We do not require you to provide your government name, postal address, or phone number to play.

3. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Operate the game, including loading positions and validating guesses;
  • Provide leaderboards, daily challenges, and related competitive features;
  • Maintain security, detect abuse, and enforce technical rate limits;
  • Improve stability and fix bugs;
  • Comply with applicable law where required.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data to show behavioural advertising through third-party ad networks as part of this App’s core design; if that ever changes, we will update this policy.
